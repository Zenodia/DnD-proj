# Chapter 06 — The Rupture War

## Purpose

Deliver the full payoff: fans get the escalating, sovereign-level confrontation they want, while the teaching model culminates in a complete agent loop.

## Story summary

The truth emerges. The rarest gates were not random disasters but pressure tests selecting for a hunter who could survive total system breakdown. The Rupture Court now forces simultaneous crises across the region: false alerts, corrupted mission specs, sabotaged proofs, broken command links, missing archives, and public panic. The player can no longer rely on raw dominance alone. To win, they must execute the entire cycle with discipline and style.

## Learning goal

Full integration of all 7 steps across all 5 layers.

## Scenes

### court_reveal
The enemy explains the logic of collapse.

### seven_fronts
Multiple missions open at once, each stressing a different step.

### shadow_march
The player deploys the full guild across the city.

### sovereign_choice
The player chooses between absolute personal power and resilient shared systems.

### final_loop
Victory comes from a complete end-to-end execution, not just the last attack.

## Choices

- Centralize every decision personally.
- Delegate through tested companions.
- Chase the main boss immediately.
- Stabilize the full mission network first.

## Win state

The player becomes a sovereign protector whose mastery is measured by reliability, not only force.

## Fail state

The boss falls, but the system collapses around the city, creating a hollow victory ending.
