# Chapter 02 — System Awakening

## Purpose

Transform the shock of survival into disciplined growth. The player learns that the System does not exist to flatter them; it exists to measure them.

## Story summary

The player wakes after the massacre with impossible messages visible only to them. Daily tasks appear. Basic gates begin exposing hidden metadata. What looked like chaos now reveals structure. The player starts to understand that power comes from following conditions, collecting the right information, and interpreting it better than everyone else.

## Learning goal

Spec-Driven Development + Collect and Enrich.

## Scenes

### infirmary_whispers
Rumors spread that the player should have died.

### first_daily_quest
A simple task becomes the first proof that the System has rules.

### training_yard
The player tests new strength against old expectations.

### metadata_gate
A minor dungeon reveals richer information than other hunters can see.

### first_spec_mission
The player wins by identifying the true objective rather than merely killing the boss.

## Choices

- Hide the System from everyone.
- Share partial information with Seo Mina.
- Follow the daily quest exactly.
- Break instructions to chase faster gains.

## Win state

The player clears the first spec-based gate and earns confidence in the System.

## Fail state

The player gains strength but misunderstands objectives, causing collateral damage or missed rewards.

## Rewards

- Quest log UI unlocked
- Threat classification unlocked
- Ally trust branch begins
- First ranked stat increase
